This Privacy Policy is designed to inform you about your rights and our obligations regarding your personal data in a clear, transparent manner.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect different types of information to provide and improve our services to you:

Personal Information

When you book a tour or contact us, we may collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Passport information (for booking purposes)
  • Nationality
  • Date of birth
  • Special requests or requirements
  • Payment information (processed securely through our payment providers)

Technical Information

When you visit our website, we automatically collect:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device type
  • Operating system
  • Time and date of your visit
  • Pages you viewed
  • Time spent on those pages
  • Referring website addresses

3. Why We Collect It

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

Booking Processing

  • To process and confirm your tour bookings
  • To communicate with you about your booking
  • To arrange necessary travel documentation

Customer Support

  • To respond to your inquiries and requests
  • To provide customer service and support
  • To send important notifications about your booking

Legal Compliance

  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To maintain business records as required by law
  • To protect our legal rights and interests

Website Improvement

  • To analyze website usage and improve user experience
  • To diagnose problems and maintain security
  • To develop new features and services

4. How We Store and Protect Data

We take the security of your personal information seriously and implement appropriate measures to protect it:

Data Security

  • We use secure servers to store your personal information
  • All data transmission between your browser and our website is encrypted using SSL technology
  • We implement access controls to limit who can access your information
  • Regular security assessments and updates to our systems

Data Retention

We keep your personal information only for as long as necessary:

  • Booking information is retained for 7 years to comply with tax and legal requirements
  • Contact form submissions are kept for 2 years
  • Technical data is typically retained for 26 months

After these periods, your data is securely deleted or anonymized.

5. Data Sharing

We share your information only when necessary to provide our services or as required by law:

Service Providers

We may share your information with trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website and business:

  • Payment processors (to process your payments)
  • Hotels and accommodation providers (to confirm your booking)
  • Transportation providers (for airport transfers and tour transportation)
  • Customer relationship management systems
  • Email service providers (for sending booking confirmations)

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by law:

  • In response to a court order or legal request
  • To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property
  • To investigate potential fraud or illegal activities

We never sell your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

6. Your Rights Under GDPR

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have several rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: You can ask us to correct any inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Right to Erasure: You can request that we delete your personal data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You can ask us to temporarily or permanently stop processing your data.
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request a copy of your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object: You can object to our processing of your data for direct marketing or legitimate interests.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You can withdraw your consent at any time where we rely on consent to process your data.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@tajvista.com. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

If you are not satisfied with our response, you have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority, such as the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) in the UK or your local data protection authority.

7. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience and provide certain features:

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly (e.g., session cookies for booking process)
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website (e.g., Google Analytics)

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • Block third-party cookies
  • Block all cookies
  • Delete cookies when you close your browser

Please note that blocking some types of cookies may impact your experience on our website and the services we can offer.

8. Policy Updates

When we make significant changes to this policy, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page
  • Place a prominent notice on our website
  • Send an email notification to users who have provided us with their email address (for material changes)

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your personal information.

Previous versions of this policy are available upon request by contacting privacy@tajvista.com.
